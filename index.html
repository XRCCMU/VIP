Variable Importance Projection

Variable Importance Projection (VIP) analysis is used to assess the importance of gut microbial metabolites in a predictive model. Metabolites with higher VIP values contributed greatly to the prediction results of the model, revealing the relationship between gut microbiota and host health.

Through VIP analysis, important metabolites related to specific diseases (such as inflammatory bowel disease, metabolic syndrome, etc.) can be identified, providing a basis for early diagnosis of diseases and biomarker screening.
